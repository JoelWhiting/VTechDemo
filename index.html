About the Research Upload and Search Features

Welcome to the Virginia Tech Additive Manufacturing Research Site! Our platform is designed to empower researchers, students, and collaborators by providing access to cutting-edge research in additive manufacturing. We aim to foster collaboration and drive innovation in the field by offering a centralized hub for sharing knowledge and resources.

  • Upload Research: Easily upload your research, data files, and other resources by navigating to the Upload page. Our streamlined form ensures that you can include all relevant metadata, making your work discoverable by others in the field.
  • Search Research: Explore our growing repository using the Search functionality. Filter results by keywords, categories, or authors to quickly find the information you need.

Join us in shaping the future of additive manufacturing by contributing your research and leveraging the wealth of knowledge shared on this platform. Together, we can accelerate progress and innovation.
